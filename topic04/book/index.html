Continuous Assessment

At the end of this double lab you must know and have started to design your XML file for your project. You must submit to me through Moodle name of your project or topic area and a description of the data you will be storing.

Here are some ideas:

  • a food menu
  • a catalog of computer parts
  • a magazine
  • a library book
  • social networking profiles
  • itunes music
  • movies
  • addressbook
  • gym membership
  • doctors patients
  • ebay auction items
  • ebay seller information
  • A bus schedule
  • A family tree (genealogy)
  • An email log
  • A recipe book
  • A menu
  • A travel catalog
  • TV-listings
  • Meeting minutes

Pick one or come up with your own and start to plan the data that is to be stored. Each of you need to do a different set of data so when you have chosen tell me and I will cross it off the available list.
